Why I Started a Non-Profit

April 25th 2026

I started this non-profit because I saw a need in my community that wasn't being met. Many people struggle to afford basic services, and I wanted to create an organization that could fill that gap. With the help of volunteers and donors, we've been able to make a real difference in people's lives. Every day, I'm reminded why this work matters.

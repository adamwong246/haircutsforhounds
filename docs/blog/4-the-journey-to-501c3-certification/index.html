The Journey to 501(c)(3) Certification

April 24th 2026

Becoming a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization was one of the most rewarding – and challenging – steps we’ve taken. The process involved drafting a detailed mission statement, filing Form 1023 with the IRS, and compiling financial projections for our first three years of operation.

We spent countless evenings reading IRS guidelines, consulting with a pro‑bono attorney, and revising our bylaws to ensure they met every requirement. After several months of paperwork and a nerve‑wracking waiting period, we finally received our determination letter in the mail.

The certification opens doors to grant funding, tax‑deductible donations, and partnerships with other nonprofits. Most importantly, it gives our supporters confidence that every dollar they give is used transparently and for the mission we all believe in. We’re proud to be a 501(c)(3) and excited for what comes next.
